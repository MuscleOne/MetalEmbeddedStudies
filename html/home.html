Object

To explore how metal fragments implanted in the human body effect our health, a series of experiments were conducted to obtain transcriptomics data from rats with metal implants. Here, we manually curate the data and present a webapp for researchers to search, download and analyze the relative data directly.

Datasets

As of 1-Nov-2021, datasets were included are as follows:

  • Data related to expression profiling
    • Expression values
    • Information on animal ID, testing sample name and treatment information such as embodied metal name, tissue type, sample collecting time
  • Data related to weight
    • Weight values
    • Information on animal ID and treatment information such as embodied metal name, euthanized time, weighting time
  • Data related to metal concentration at euthanasia
    • Concentration of a metal (say Ni) in a tissue in rats embodied with this metal
    • Concentration of a metal (say Ni) in a tissue in rats embodied with Ta
    • Three types of tissues: serum, stomach, urine
    • Concentration of creatinine for animal euthanasia

Functions

Users can find there are three sub-items of the query tab in the sidebar panel: Expression, Weight and Concentration. Download is available once query results are obtained.

  • In the Expression item, one can search both expression matrix and the metadata of the samples, by given conditions of library, metal embedded and the collected month of the sample.
  • In the weight item, one can search the weight of the rats, by given conditions of metal embedded, the euthanized period, and the measure time points.
  • In the concentration item, one can search the weight of the rats, by given conditions of metal embedded, the euthanized period, and the measure types.
